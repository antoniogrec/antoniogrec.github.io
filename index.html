Welcome To Avral Legal

Our firm focuses primarily on Italian law, American law, and the international laws that regulate the relationship between the United States and most of Europe. Our lawyers are experts in family law in both Italy and the USA. Our lawyers manage matters involving legal separation, divorce, separation when not married, child custody, child support, spousal support and alimony, domestic violence, adoption and parental kidnapping (Hague convention).

Our attorneys also manage matters of general civil litigation, including employment law, estate management (will, trusts, inheritance & probate proceedings), immigration, real estate, and criminal law. Laws are very often much more complex than you understood. Matters become even more complex and justice more elusive when international elements are present or you must manage the case from afar. We are here to help you.
